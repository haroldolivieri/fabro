| Similar name | **Favro** (favro.com) — dev team PM platform. One letter off. | High |
| GitHub | Username `fabro` taken (academic researcher) | Low |
| iOS App | FaBro Browser — cloud file manager | Low |
| USPTO | "MR. FABRO" trademark (consulting); Fabro International LLC | Low |

## Recommendation

**Sigla** and **Veriko** are the strongest candidates — both have .dev domains secured and the cleanest conflict profiles.

- **Sigla** is the stronger _brand_ — 5 chars, distinctive, memorable, and "a seal of quality" is a compelling narrative for a verification tool. sigla.dev is the primary domain.

- **Veriko** is the _safest_ choice — fewest conflicts, most unregistered TLDs available for defensive registration, and inherently more defensible as a trademark (invented word). veriko.dev is the primary domain.

- **Facto** has the best marketing hook ("the de facto standard") but lacks a .dev domain. facto.sh or facto.tools would be the primary, which is unconventional.

- **Fabro** sounds great but the one-letter Favro proximity is a real hazard, and it also lacks a .dev domain.
